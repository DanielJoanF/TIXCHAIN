# TixChain — Business Model & Feasibility

---

## Revenue Streams

| # | Sumber Pendapatan | Model | Estimasi |
|---|------------------|-------|----------|
| 1 | **Primary Sale Fee** | 5% dari setiap penjualan tiket pertama | Split: 4% promoter, 1% platform |
| 2 | **Secondary Market Fee** | 3% dari setiap resale | 2% royalty promoter, 1% platform |
| 3 | **SaaS Dashboard** | Subscription untuk event organizer | Rp 500K-2M/bulan (Pro Plan) |
| 4 | **White-label** | Licensing teknologi ke venue/festival besar | Custom pricing |

## Unit Economics

```
Contoh: Event konser 1000 tiket @ Rp 650.000

Primary Sale Revenue:
  Total penjualan:     Rp 650.000.000
  Platform fee (1%):   Rp   6.500.000
  Promoter fee (4%):   Rp  26.000.000

Secondary Market (estimasi 20% tiket dijual kembali = 200 tiket):
  Avg resale price:    Rp 715.000 (110% cap)
  Total resale volume: Rp 143.000.000
  Platform fee (1%):   Rp   1.430.000
  Promoter royalty (2%): Rp 2.860.000

Total Platform Revenue per Event:
  Primary:   Rp 6.500.000
  Secondary: Rp 1.430.000
  Total:     Rp 7.930.000
```

## Cost Structure

| Item | Estimasi | Catatan |
|------|----------|---------|
| Gas Fee (Polygon) | $0.01-0.02/tx | Sangat murah, ~Rp 160/transaksi |
| Server Backend | Rp 500K-2M/bulan | Cloud hosting (AWS/GCP) |
| Smart Contract Audit | Rp 15-50M (one-time) | Keamanan critical |
| Development | Tim 3-5 orang | Fullstack + Blockchain dev |
| Marketing | Rp 5-10M/bulan | Partnership + social media |

## Competitive Advantage

| TixChain | Kompetitor (Tiket.com, Loket) |
|----------|------------------------------|
| ✅ Anti-calo price cap (immutable on-chain) | ❌ Tidak ada proteksi harga |
| ✅ Tiket = NFT (tidak bisa dipalsukan) | ❌ Tiket bisa di-screenshot |
| ✅ Dynamic QR 15 detik | ❌ Static QR bisa di-copy |
| ✅ Promoter royalty otomatis | ❌ Promoter rugi di secondary |
| ✅ Transparansi blockchain | ❌ Black box |

## Legal Considerations

- Blockchain-based ticketing legal di Indonesia (bukan crypto trading)
- Comply dengan UU Perlindungan Konsumen (transparansi harga)
- NFT sebagai bukti kepemilikan digital (bukan investasi)
- Data privacy sesuai UU PDP
